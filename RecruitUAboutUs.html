About Us - RecruitU

Our Story

Welcome to RecruitU, where innovation meets excellence, and our goal is to help make recruiting for every level of sport as easy as possible for everyone.

Our Mission

At RecruitU, we aim to make the recruitment process as easy as possible for everyone. We deal with all levels of sports, and allow users to make innovative pages about themselves to get themselves out there.

Our Vision

We envision a future where everyone has an equal chance at being recruited, and no one feels like they were left without a chance. We are very passionate about sports here at RecruitU, and plan to keep on working towards our ideal future.
