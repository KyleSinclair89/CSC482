About Us - RecruitU

Our Story

Welcome to RecruitU, where innovation meets excellence, and our goal is to help make recruiting for every level of sport as easy as possible for everyone.

Our Mission

At RecruitU, we aim to make the recruitment process as easy as possible for everyone. We deal with all levels of sports, and allow users to make innovative pages about themselves to get themselves out there.

Our Vision

We at RecruitU envision a future where the doors of opportunity swing wide open for everyone, regardless of background or circumstance. Our fervent belief is that every individual deserves an equal shot at success, without ever feeling marginalized or overlooked. Driven by our unwavering passion for sports, we are committed to fostering an inclusive environment where talent knows no boundaries. With each stride forward, we tirelessly strive towards our vision of a world where meritocracy reigns supreme, and every aspiring athlete is empowered to reach their full potential. Our dedication to this noble cause fuels our relentless pursuit of excellence, as we continue to push the boundaries of possibility. Together, let us champion fairness, diversity, and inclusivity, ensuring that the playing field is level for all who dare to dream. At RecruitU, the pursuit of greatness knows no limits.

Kyle is versed in many different coding languages, including but not limited to: Java, Python, C, C++, SQL, JavaScript, HTML, PHP, CSS, and more. Kyle's goal when starting RecruitU was the same as the company insists: To make recruiting in all levels of sports as fair, and easy, as possible. Kyle is very passionate about his role at RecruitU, and wishes to continue to watch his role expand.
